Web Developer - Brand Maker

About Brand Maker

Brand Maker is a leading digital solutions company specializing in website and mobile app development, business systems, and digital marketing. With branches across Egypt, we serve clients across various sectors by delivering innovative, customized, and result-driven digital products.

Job Description

We are hiring a passionate Web Developer to join our growing team. You will be responsible for developing dynamic, user-friendly websites and ensuring they are fast, secure, and optimized for user experience. If you enjoy working on challenging projects and bringing creative ideas to life, this is the role for you.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.
  • Customize WordPress themes and plugins according to project requirements.
  • Collaborate with designers, marketers, and backend developers.
  • Ensure websites are optimized for speed, SEO, and cross-browser compatibility.
  • Debug and troubleshoot issues across different browsers and devices.
  • Integrate third-party tools such as payment gateways, APIs, and CRMs.

Requirements

  • At least 2 years of experience as a web developer.
  • Strong knowledge of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and PHP.
  • Experience working with WordPress is a must.
  • Familiarity with Git, APIs, and MySQL databases.
  • Good problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work remotely and meet deadlines.

Work Details

  • Remote position (with optional monthly in-office visit).
  • Full-time or part-time depending on availability.
  • Flexible hours with clear task tracking and communication.

Benefits

  • Work-from-home opportunity.
  • Competitive salary with project-based bonuses.
  • Supportive and innovative work culture.
  • Ongoing learning and growth opportunities.

  • Cairo
  • Alexandria
