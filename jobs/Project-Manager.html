Job Description

We are looking for a proactive and organized Project Manager to lead and coordinate projects across our teams. The ideal candidate will ensure timely delivery, maintain clear communication with clients, and manage internal workflows efficiently.

Key Responsibilities

  • Plan, initiate, and manage web, app, and marketing projects.
  • Coordinate between clients, developers, designers, and marketing teams.
  • Ensure deadlines are met and project objectives are achieved.
  • Track progress using tools like Trello, Zoho, or similar platforms.
  • Identify risks and resolve project issues proactively.
  • Provide regular updates to management and clients.

Requirements

  • Proven experience as a Project Manager (at least 2 years).
  • Excellent communication and time-management skills.
  • Familiarity with project management tools (e.g., Zoho, Trello, ClickUp).
  • Strong problem-solving and leadership abilities.
  • Ability to work remotely with minimal supervision.
  • Fluency in English and Arabic is a plus.

Work Details

  • Remote position (with optional monthly in-office meeting).
  • Full-time or part-time based on agreement.
  • Flexible schedule with performance-based tracking.

Benefits

  • Work-from-home flexibility.
  • Competitive salary + performance bonuses.
  • Supportive and creative work environment.
  • Monthly team-building sessions and continuous learning opportunities.
